About Us

For true diamond cognoscenti, there is only one name that stands for unwavering trust and unparalleled brilliance. Sunny Diamonds. Hallmarked as the go-to destination for the perfect diamond experience, we are the only retail jeweller who offers Internally Flawless (IF) Diamonds on the entire spectrum of diamond jewellery today.


Powered by an incessant commitment to present collections with world-class quality, we offer exceptional IF diamond jewellery to our customers while adhering to standards of creativity and quality. Each of our masterpieces boasts of a flawless craftsmanship and timeless magnificence. Presenting a wide range of Internally Flawless Diamonds sourced directly from Belgium, we ensure BIS Hallmark for our wide-ranging selections that have received Quality Certifications from three of the Most Trusted Diamond Grading laboratories in the world - Gemological Institute of America (GIA), American Gem Society (AGS) and Hoge Raad Voor Diamant (HRD) Antwerp. And that’s not all! The diamonds sold by us are certified Conflict-Free in compliance with the Kimberley Process and the UN resolutions.

We know what our customers want. It’s as simple as that. Every piece handcrafted by our master artisans with love and care is a work of art. Because we know the true glory of a diamond lies in reflecting the inner glow of the person who wears it. And that’s precisely why Sunny Diamonds remains the first choice for diamond enthusiasts, a fact evidenced by the positive reception we have got from customers from all quarters. As a matter of fact, we are the go-to jeweller for some of the world’s biggest celebrities, millionaires, athletes, socialites and royalties.


To create the most exquisite adornments and emblems of our beliefs with the utmost certainty of everlasting value – That’s the promise we’ve made as a brand to our customers. We believe that our mission to preserve the tradition of symbolic and valued jewellery, hand-crafted by masters of excellence, depends on us fulfilling this promise.
